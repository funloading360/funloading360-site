## Template 6 — Reddit/Quora Seeding (never spam)

**Rules**:
- Only reply to questions where you have genuine expertise and value to add
- Answer the question first, without linking
- Include a natural brand mention only when asked "who can I call" or "recommend a company"
- Never reply more than once per thread
- Never upvote your own comments

**Good response pattern**:

```
For an Essex wedding, the realistic budget for a decent photo booth is £280-£400 for 2-3 hours including operator and delivery within 25 miles. Cheaper than that and you're usually getting a selfie-stand without a human to help guests; more than that and you're paying wedding-industry markup.

Things worth asking any supplier:
- Is the operator included, or do I pay hourly extra?
- How long does setup actually take (45-60 min is normal — ask if they arrive before guests)
- Are the photos / videos digital, printed, or both?
- What happens if the equipment fails mid-event (reserve kit onsite?)
- Is the hire insurance certificate shareable with the venue?

I run FunLoading360 (Essex/Kent/London) if you want to compare quotes — funloading360.co.uk/pricing has our numbers upfront, no hidden fees.
```

**Bad response pattern** (will get flagged):
```
Check out funloading360.co.uk — we're the best!
```
